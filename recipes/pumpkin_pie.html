Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Description

This homemade pumpkin pie made with mashed, cooked pumpkin is my family's favorite. Skip the canned pumpkin and store-bought crust and make your holiday pumpkin pie from scratch with a flaky homemade pastry crust and a deliciously spiced pumpkin filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed, cooked pie pumpkin
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Make the pastry crust: Mix flour and salt together in a bowl.
  3. Shape dough into a ball with lightly floured hands.
